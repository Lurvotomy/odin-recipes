Garlic Bread

Return

This Garlic Bread is simple and tasty - and no making bread is required!

It has fresh herbs, tons of flavorful roasted garlic, and is perfectly toasted.

This easy garlic bread recipe is delicious served with your fav pasta dishes & comfort foods, and is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser!

Ingredients

To get started on the Pizza, we will first need the below ingredients:

  • Bread: I like to use Italian bread or a loaf of French bread (instead of a baguette) to get those nice thick slices.

  • Butter: softened butter, which will melt into all of the bread nooks & crannies - salted or unsalted butter.(I use 1/2 cup of butter)

  • Garlic: Fresh garlic cloves

  • Seasoning: Italian Herbs

Cooking Steps

  1. Preheat oven. Set to 200°C/400°F

  2. Prep your pan & bread. Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper or tinfoil. Cut bread into halves


  3. Garlic Butter. In a small bowl mix the herbs and salt together with the softened butter. Spread it evenly over the top of the bread halves.

  4. Time to cook. Bake the bread for 10-15 minutes until it’s slightly golden brown on the edges.

Remove from the oven, and enjoy on it's own or paired with your favourite Italian dish!
